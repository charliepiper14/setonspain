Critical Issues Identified

Issue 1: Budget Constraint Limiting Growth

47.84% of impressions lost due to budget (up 992% from 4.38% prior period). Your ads qualify to show but budget runs out before capturing available demand. hihomes.com.es is capturing these impressions.

Impact: Approximately 8,400 additional impressions lost daily to budget constraints.

Issue 2: True CPA 150% Above Target

€82.39 actual lead form CPA versus €32-33 target. Platform metrics obscure this gap by including engagement events as conversions.

Impact: Each lead costs €49-50 more than target, reducing ROI on €35,000 average commission.

Issue 3: Weekend Performance Collapse

Saturday Nov 22 showed €109.51 CPA spike. Weekend conversion rates consistently lower than weekday performance. Saturday and Sunday combined produced limited lead forms relative to spend.

Impact: Approximately €200-300 weekly spend during low-conversion periods.

Issue 4: Underperforming Campaigns Consuming Budget

Bayside Homes (€62.90 CPA, 1 lead), Amaranta Living (€47.41 CPA, 1 lead), and DSA test (€53.15 spend, 0 leads) collectively spent €163 with minimal returns.

Impact: €163 could generate 10+ additional impressions on proven performers.

Issue 5: Desktop Inefficiency

Desktop generating fewer lead forms at higher cost than mobile. No bid adjustment currently in place to optimize device mix.

Impact: Estimated €15-20 weekly in suboptimal device allocation.
