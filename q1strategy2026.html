Housing Policy Compliance Strategy

Proven methodology rollout for Q1 2026

Los Flamingos Success Case Study

The Four Seasons Los Flamingos ad was successfully approved in Q4 after implementing compliant ad copy methodology. This approach generated 9.08 conversions at €23.74 CPA during Q4, validating the strategy.

Non-Compliant Language (Avoid)
  • "From POA" or any price qualifiers
  • "Secure Your Dream Home Today"
  • "Get Floor Plans Today!"
  • "Family-friendly" or demographic terms
  • "Limited Time" or urgency language
  • "Perfect for retirement"
  • "Ideal for families"
Compliant Language (Use)
  • "Request latest listings"
  • "Request Available Apartments"
  • "Book A Viewing"
  • "Contact Us for Information"
  • "Explore Available Properties"
  • "Schedule a Property Tour"
  • "View Development Details"

Q1 Compliance Rollout Plan

1
Week 1-2: Audit and Documentation

Complete audit of all ad groups for housing policy violations. Document all ads requiring updates.

2
Week 3-4: Priority Campaign Updates

Update High and Medium campaign ads using approved Los Flamingos methodology. Submit for review.

3
Week 5-6: Secondary Campaign Rollout

Apply compliant copy to Low campaign, standalone campaigns, and any new ad groups.

4
Ongoing: Monitoring and Appeals

Monitor approval status, submit appeals for any rejections, and document learnings for future reference.

Expected Impact

Full housing policy compliance will unlock restricted ad inventory, potentially increasing available impressions by 15-20% and reducing lost impression share due to ad eligibility issues.
