Core Web Vitals: Where This Fits

Current Status

All 30 tested landing pages continue to fail mobile CWV. Average mobile performance score remains around 25/100 with average page load exceeding 6 seconds.

Funnel Labs Update

Tony and Emma have received the Funnel Labs quote for CWV remediation. Under review alongside the option of a full website rebuild. WordPress access will be shared with Kenny for CMS and theme audit once scope is confirmed.

Awaiting confirmation to proceed. This is the single biggest improvement available for reducing CPC, improving ad rank, and closing the position gap at lower cost.

Why CWV Matters Right Now

Mobile delivers 73.6% of all conversions at roughly one-third the CPA of desktop. Every improvement to mobile page speed feeds directly into Quality Score, which reduces the bid required to hold abs. top of page. With hihomes still ahead of us on Medium campaign position, the CWV fix is what closes that gap at a lower ongoing cost rather than through bid increases alone.
