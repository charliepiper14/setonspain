Los Flamingos success methodology & rollout strategy

‚úÖ Los Flamingos Ad Approved - Key Changes Identified

The Four Seasons Los Flamingos ad was successfully approved after removing housing policy violations on October 20, 2025.

‚ùå RESTRICTED Ad (Paused)
Description:
"Luxury Apartments in Golf Resort Setting. From POA. Get Floor Plans Today!"

Headlines include:
"Secure Your Dream Home Today"

Violations:

  • "From POA" - Discriminatory pricing language
  • "Get Floor Plans Today!" - Overly urgent promotional CTA
  • "Secure Your Dream Home Today" - Emotional/aspirational language
‚úÖ APPROVED Ad (Active)
Description:
"Luxury Apartments in Golf Resort Setting. Request latest listings today!"

Headlines include:
"Request Available Apartments"
"Book A Viewing"

Compliant Changes:

  • Removed all pricing references (POA)
  • Changed to neutral "Request latest listings"
  • Removed emotional urgency language
  • Focused on factual property features

üìã Housing Policy Compliance Rules

‚ùå Avoid These:

  • Price qualifiers (From, Starting at, POA)
  • Urgent CTAs (Today, Now, Limited Time)
  • Emotional language (Dream Home, Perfect)
  • Family-specific (Family-friendly)
  • Promotional urgency (Don't Miss Out)

‚úÖ Use Instead:

  • Request Information, Contact Us
  • Schedule Viewing, Enquire Now
  • Latest Availability, Current Listings
  • Property Features, Amenities
  • Location Benefits, Development Info
